# Artwork Glossary - Bharatvarsh Website

This document lists all required images with their positions, purposes, and specifications. Portrait variants are needed for orientation-responsive design.

| Context | Landscape | Portrait |

| Modal/Card images | 16:9 (1600×900px) | 3:4 (1200×1600px) |
| Character cards | 4:3 (1200×900px) | 3:4 (900×1200px) |

## Visual Style Guidelines

1. **Color Palette**: Match the Obsidian Aurora theme
   - Dark backgrounds (#0F1419, #1A1F2E)
   - Accent highlights (mustard #F1C232, powder blue #C9DBEE)

2. **Mood**: Atmospheric, cinematic, mysterious
   - Heavy shadows, dramatic lighting
   - Film grain effect where appropriate

3. **Composition**:
   - Landscape: Wide establishing shots, rule of thirds
   - Portrait: Vertical emphasis, subject centered or top-third

4. **File Optimization**:
   - WebP format at 80-85% quality
   - Maximum file size: 500KB for backgrounds, 200KB for cards
